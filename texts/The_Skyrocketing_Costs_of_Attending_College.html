The Skyrocketing Costs of Attending College

https://economix.blogs.nytimes.com/2009/10/20/the-skyrocketing-costs-of-attending-college/

My colleagues over at The Choice are soliciting comments on a new College Board report. The report found that the the average published (sticker price) annual cost of tuition and fees at four-year public colleges rose 6.5 percent from last year, to $7,020. Tuition rose across the board at other education institutions, too. To put this in perspective, we’ve (surprise!) whipped up some charts using these new numbers.
