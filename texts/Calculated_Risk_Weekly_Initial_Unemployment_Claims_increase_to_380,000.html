Calculated Risk: Weekly Initial Unemployment Claims increase to 380,000

http://www.calculatedriskblog.com/2012/04/weekly-initial-unemployment-claims_12.html

The DOL reports: In the week ending April 7, the advance figure for seasonally adjusted initial claims was 380,000, an increase of 13,000 from the previous week's revised figure of 367,000. The 4-week moving average was 368,500, an increase of 4,250 from the previous week's revised average of 364,250. Click on graph for larger image.
