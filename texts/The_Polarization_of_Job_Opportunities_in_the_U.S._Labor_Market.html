Community Investments Fall 2011 « Past issues Community Investments Vol 23, Issue 2 The Polarization of Job Opportunities in the U.S. Labor Market Author(s): David Autor, Massachusetts Institute of Technology An in-depth analysis of the state of the U.S. labor market over the past three decades reveals that the U.S. labor market is polarizing into low- and high-skill jobs, with fewer opportunities in the middle.
