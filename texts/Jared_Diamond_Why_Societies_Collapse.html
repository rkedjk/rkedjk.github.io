Jared Diamond: Why Societies Collapse

http://www.ritholtz.com/blog/2009/01/jared-diamond-why-societies-collapse/

Why do societies fail? With lessons from the Norse of Iron Age Greenland, deforested Easter Island and present-day Montana, Jared Diamond talks about the signs that collapse is near, and how — if we see it in time — we can prevent it. Note that he mentions the conflict of interests — when Elites “insulate themselves from the consequences of their decisions, advancing their own short term interests against the interests of overall society.” Filmed Feb 2003
