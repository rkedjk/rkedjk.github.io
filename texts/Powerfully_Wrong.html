Powerfully Wrong

https://krugman.blogs.nytimes.com/2013/11/18/powerfully-wrong/

Brad DeLong continues the pile-on over Barry Ritholtz’s roll of dishonor — the people who signed the currency debasement letter and haven’t shown any sign of learning anything from being completely wrong. So I did a quick cross-reference, and discovered that three of the ignoble crew are on Worth Magazine’s list of the 100 most powerful people in finance. That is, surely, a bit dispiriting. On the other hand, maybe that list itself is another roll of dishonor. If you ask me, anyone who made that list should automatically be considered a person of dubious character. Especially people around rank 96 or so.
