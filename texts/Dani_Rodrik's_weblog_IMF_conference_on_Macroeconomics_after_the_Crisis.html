Dani Rodrik's weblog: IMF conference on Macroeconomics after the Crisis

http://rodrik.typepad.com/dani_rodriks_weblog/2011/03/imf-conference-on-macroeconomics-after-the-crisis.html

I spoke today at a panel on economic growth after the crisis at a conference organized by the IMF. My co-panelists were George Akerlof, Mike Spence, Andrew Sheng, and Paul Romer. In the audience: Bob Solow, Joe Stiglitz, Olivier Blanchard, and many others. In retrospect, it was probably a mistake to have a slide called "Growth Theory in One Slide."
