And people did not only lost trust in politicians, but in media, medicine, science and what not. Basically in every authority. It is by the way not that hard to see why. That is the situation in which a lot of Germans were told that some countries needed financial help from Germany. And if, in the European mindset this was even okay for him, in 2015 he needed to pay for over 1 million refugees. While at the same time seeing the streets flooded with homeless from rumania and Bulgaria. The reason why populism is so successful is because the established "Elite" failed completely.
