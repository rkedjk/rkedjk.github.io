Have the BRIC Nations Lost Their Momentum?

Introduction Dhiraj Singh/Bloomberg News This week, China released data that showed its economy was continuing to weaken. Meanwhile India’s economy shows signs of slowing, as do Brazil’s and Russia’s. As our attention focuses on Europe’s troubled economy, are we in danger of ignoring a slowdown in the BRIC nations — Brazil, Russia, India and China — that could have a huge impact on the global economy? What happens if one or all of the BRIC countries come to a halt?
