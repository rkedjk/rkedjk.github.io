Interview with Andrew Ross Sorkin: 'Wall Street Bankers Are Not Pure Evil'

http://www.spiegel.de/international/business/0,1518,712498,00.html

Interview with Andrew Ross Sorkin 'Wall Street Bankers Are Not Pure Evil' US journalist Andrew Ross Sorkin speaks to SPIEGEL about his book, "Too Big to Fail," the inside story of Wall Street and the financial crisis. He talks about the lack of remorse on the part of many CEOs and addresses the question of whether he got too close to the country's top bankers.
