Where Do Your Tax Dollars Go?

Wondering where your federal tax dollars go? This chart, from the Center for Budget and Policy Priorities, helps lay out the answer: Center for Budget and Policy Priorities I have some quibbles with the category names — for example, I would consider both Social Security and Medicaid part of the country’s “social safety net” — but still, the chart helps provide some perspective. Here is a comparable chart that the center, a liberal-leaning research organization in Washington, created last spring to illustrate the destination of state tax dollars: Center for Budget and Policy Priorities The state numbers are averages, and the spending mix varies greatly from state to state. For example, Alaska and West Virginia each spend 11 percent of their respective budgets on K-12 education, while Michigan spends 31 percent, according to the center.
