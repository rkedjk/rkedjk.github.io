Calculated Risk: Weekly Initial Unemployment Claims decline below 400,000

http://www.calculatedriskblog.com/2011/11/weekly-initial-unemployment-claims.html?

The DOL reports: In the week ending October 29, the advance figure for seasonally adjusted initial claims was 397,000, a decrease of 9,000 from the previous week's revised figure of 406,000. The 4-week moving average was 404,500, a decrease of 2,000 from the previous week's revised average of 406,500. Click on graph for larger image.
