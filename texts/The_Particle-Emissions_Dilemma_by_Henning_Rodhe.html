The Particle-Emissions Dilemma by Henning Rodhe

https://www.project-syndicate.org/commentary/the-particle-emissions-dilemma

I am sorry but the suggestions in the article, and the proposed management completely misunderstands the problem. It is like we have a very sick patient, with potentially terminal disease and we only care about reducing the fever, because the fever is disturbing us, as this what we can see and measure. If we only care about the fever, without meaningful treatment for the illness. the disease spreads further finally killing the patient. Why do we not examine the reason why we emit so many particles? The article is right, leaders of countries do not intentionally emit particles to cause harm to their own people and to the environment. We emit so many particles because of our present excessive lifestyle, when we want to devour and exploit everything and anything we can. The Asian or African countries are simply playing "catch up" to the wealthier western counterparts, who have done their own share of polluting before, but now demand the developing countries to stop doing it. The true disease is our present socio-economic model of unnecessary and unnatural overproduction/over consumption, making and buying goods we simply do not need for a natural and comfortable human life, we are tricked into "wanting" those luxuries by the sophisticated marketing machinery. This lifestyle and attitude is causing each and every social and environmental problem we are facing today. If we truly want to reduce the emission particles, and we truly want to return to a healthier lifestyle we need to return to necessity and resource based life. And in order to do that willingly, consciously finally we have to provide people with unbiased facts, and information about the system we live in, about the true causes why we entered our present global crisis with all of its elements, and how we could re-balance our living system with human input.
