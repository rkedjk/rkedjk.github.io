Centrist figure at the Federal Reserve to step down next year

https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/wonk/wp/2016/09/13/centrist-figure-at-the-federal-reserve-to-step-down-next-year/

On Monday, a day before announcing his retirement, Lockhart reiterated his call for a “serious discussion” at the Fed’s next meeting about whether to raise its target interest rate. He noted that inflation remains weak and said that indications that it might be picking up were not “overwhelmingly convincing.” But he said the economy appeared to be “chugging along, not stalling out,” and highlighted strong hiring in the job market.
