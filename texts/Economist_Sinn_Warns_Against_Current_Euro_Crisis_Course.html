Interview with Economist Hans-Werner Sinn 'Temporary Euro-Zone Exit Would Stabilize Greece' In a SPIEGEL interview, pugnacious German economist Hans-Werner Sinn warns of the huge dangers associated with a continuation of current bailout policies, why he believes Greece and Portugal should temporarily leave the euro zone and why financial markets are anything but irrational.
