The Chicken Little Scale

https://krugman.blogs.nytimes.com/2013/08/12/the-chicken-little-scale/?gwh=8933C83D6E15B707539C2955ED885BF0

Brad DeLong sends us to Dan Drezner, who rates Hubbard-Kane along what he calls the Cassandra Scale of excessive debt phobia. But while I like the scale, Drezner kind of messes up his mythology; after all, the point about Cassandra was that although nobody would believe her, she was always right — whereas the people he’s talking about are always wrong. I hereby propose rechristening it the Chicken Little scale.
