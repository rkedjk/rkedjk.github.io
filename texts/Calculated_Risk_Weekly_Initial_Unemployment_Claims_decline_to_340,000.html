Calculated Risk: Weekly Initial Unemployment Claims decline to 340,000

https://feedproxy.google.com/~r/CalculatedRisk/~3/MzqNhpYg6d0/weekly-initial-unemployment-claims_23.html

The DOL reports: In the week ending May 18, the advance figure for seasonally adjusted initial claims was 340,000, a decrease of 23,000 from the previous week's revised figure of 363,000. The 4-week moving average was 339,500, a decrease of 500 from the previous week's revised average of 340,000. Click on graph for larger image.
