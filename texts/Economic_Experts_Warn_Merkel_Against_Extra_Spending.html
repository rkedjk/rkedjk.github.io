Austerity vs. Popularity Economic Experts Warn Merkel on Extra Spending Earlier this week, the German chancellor agreed to extra spending that will likely to quiet some noisy elements in her coalition government ahead of next year's general election. Economic experts, however, are warning Angela Merkel to keep a tighter hold on Berlin's purse strings.
