Robert Waldmann | August 5, 2013 12:21 pm The notorious GSE bailout will probably cause the US Federal debt to be considerably lower than it would have been without the bailout. It could cause the US Federal debt to be much lower than it would have been, but that won’t be allowed to happen. Fannie and Freddie are now profitable. They are likely to pay their debts to the Treasury which would imply that the bailout will reduce the Federal debt, because they pay the Treasury much higher interest rates than the Treasury pays anyone, because they have also paid dividends, because Fannie and Freddie will pay corporate income tax (eventually) and because even after the debt to the Treasury is repaid, the Treasury will own 80% of the equity of Fannie and Freddie.
