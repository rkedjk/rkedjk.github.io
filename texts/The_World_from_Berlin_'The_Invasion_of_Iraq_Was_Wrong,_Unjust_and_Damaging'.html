The World from Berlin 'The Invasion of Iraq Was Wrong, Unjust and Damaging' Has American credibility been severely damaged by the Iraq War? One day after the last US combat unit left the country, some in the German press say that it has. Commentators are pessimistic about Iraq's ability to cope alone.
