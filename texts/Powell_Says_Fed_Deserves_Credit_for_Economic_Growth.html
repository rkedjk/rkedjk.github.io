Powell Says Fed Deserves Credit for Economic Growth

https://www.nytimes.com/2018/11/14/business/federal-reserve-powell-economy.html?partner=rss&emc=rss

DALLAS — Jerome H. Powell, the chairman of the Federal Reserve, on Wednesday countered President Trump’s loud and repeated attacks on the Fed without mentioning Mr. Trump by name. Mr. Powell hammered on two simple themes during an hourlong question-and-answer session in front of several hundred people at the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas. The Fed is doing its job, he said, and the economy is doing really well. “I’m very happy about the state of the economy now,” Mr. Powell said. “There’s pretty good reason to think we’re going to continue in a positive vein like that.” He added that the Fed deserved credit for its role in the long and steady expansion. “Our policy is part of the reason the economy is in such a good place now,” he said.
