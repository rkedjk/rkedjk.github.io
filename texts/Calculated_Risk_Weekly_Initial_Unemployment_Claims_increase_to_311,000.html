Calculated Risk: Weekly Initial Unemployment Claims increase to 311,000

http://www.calculatedriskblog.com/2014/08/weekly-initial-unemployment-claims_14.html

The DOL reports: In the week ending August 9, the advance figure for seasonally adjusted initial claims was 311,000, an increase of 21,000 from the previous week's revised level. The previous week's level was revised up by 1,000 from 289,000 to 290,000. The 4-week moving average was 295,750, an increase of 2,000 from the previous week's revised average. The previous week's average was revised up by 250 from 293,500 to 293,750. There were no special factors impacting this week's initial claims. Click on graph for larger image.
