Crime scars: can recessions produce career criminals?

http://cep.lse.ac.uk/_new/publications/abstract.asp?index=4699

Recessions typically raise youth unemployment and leave longterm labour market ‘scars’ on the young people concerned. Research by Brian Bell, Anna Bindler and Stephen Machin finds that they also have a disturbing and substantial impact in launching criminal careers.
