An interview with Nicolas Maduro, January 2019, can be read here: https://venezuelanalysis.com/interviews/14209 -- The journalist prefaces the article saying in the past year and a half the opposition has lost five national elections. The MUD opposition won in 2015 with a 58% to 41% majority in the National Assembly. It failed to enact reforms and concentrated on ousting Maduro, and then imploded. The opposition clearly has criminally sabotaged the nation. Not being willing to work in cooperation seems to be the enemy of the situation. The chief offender is the party Mr. Hausmann supports. It's good to see most of the comments on his article are critical of his positions. Here is a brief quote from an opposition leader after the failure at the polls in 2017: ""They failed in their proposal for insurrection - what do I mean when I say insurrection well you remember the speech on the 3rd of July in Chacao. The four key phrases used then were - national uprising, upsurging rebellion, zero hour and general strike. That's a proposal for insurrection. The insurrection failed, they failed at these past election and on top of that they don't like dialogue. So if you don't want to vote, you're bad at insurrection, and you don't wan't to dialogue, what do you want - collective suicide?"" See : https://venezuelanalysis.com/analysis/13452
